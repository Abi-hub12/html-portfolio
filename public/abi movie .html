The Best Movies According to Abi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Devil Wears Prada

I love this movie for its sharp wit and incredible performances, especially Meryl Streep’s commanding role as Miranda Priestly. The fashion is breathtaking, and it’s such a fun yet insightful take on ambition and personal growth.

Divergent

I enjoy Divergent for its thrilling action and Tris's journey of self-discovery. The idea of breaking free from societal labels is inspiring, and the dystopian world is fascinating to explore.

Encanto

I adore Encanto for its vibrant animation, memorable songs, and heartwarming story about family and individuality. It’s so joyful and touching—it always leaves me smiling.
